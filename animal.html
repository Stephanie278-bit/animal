Red snake

  • Breed Group : Red coral snake
  • Average Length: 18-30 in (46-76 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 14yrs
  • Weight: 2-5 pounds (0.9-2.3 kg)

Red Coral snakes are slender snakes with small, blunt heads, rounded noses, smooth scales, and brightly colored bands of red, yellow, orange, black, pink, blue, or white stripes. For instance, Micrurus fulvius, more commonly known as the Eastern coral snake, has thin black, red, and yellow bands around its body.
